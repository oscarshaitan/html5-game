NEON DEFENSE

OPERATIONS MANUAL V2.2

01. MISSION OBJECTIVE

Protect the CORE. Enemies enter through active rifts and move toward the center of the sector. You begin with 20 lives; when lives reach zero, the run ends.

  • Starting economy: 100 credits.
  • Every kill grants credits and +1 energy.
  • Prep windows between waves are your build and planning phase.

02. CONTROLS

  • Q / W / E / R Select tower type (Basic / Rapid / Sniper / Arc)
  • Left Click Select tower/base/rift, or select an empty tile to open the build panel at that location
  • Quick Build Flow After placing a tower, that tower is auto-selected so you can upgrade/sell immediately
  • U Upgrade selected tower
  • Delete / Backspace Sell selected tower
  • 1 EMP targeting mode
  • 2 Overclock targeting mode
  • Esc Deselect current target or open pause menu
  • Mouse Drag Pan camera
  • Mouse Wheel Zoom camera
  • Start Wave Button Skip prep timer and launch immediately
  • Recenter Button Snap camera back to Core
  • Touch Drag Pan camera (mobile)
  • Pinch Zoom camera (mobile)

04. CORE PLATFORM

Select the core to repair lives and upgrade the home turret platform.

  • Repair Cost: getRepairCost() starts at $50 and scales upward beyond baseline; each purchase currently restores +2 lives.
  • Core Upgrade: available up to Level 10; each purchase currently increments core level by +2 in this build.
  • Base Turret Scaling: Damage 20 + 10*(level-1), Range 150 + 30*(level-1), Cooldown max(8, 35 - 5*level).

06. WAVE & RIFT SYSTEM

Rift Topology Rules

  • Rift paths are orthogonal (no diagonal segments).
  • Paths cannot cross existing paths and cannot fold over themselves.
  • Rifts and paths cannot be placed over hardpoint cells.
  • Once a path enters Zone 0, it must continue to core without exiting Zone 0.
  • Core approach uses six hardpoint gaps: one inner lane per gap; additional paths on the same gap must merge before Zone 0.

Wave Scaling & Rift Progression

  • Wave Size: 5 + floor(wave * 2.5).
  • Rift Expansion: extra rifts are generated on waves 11/21/31/41/51, then every 5 waves after 50 (56/61/66...).
  • Veteran Rift Tiers: from wave 51 onward, each rift has a 10% per-wave chance to gain a tier.

07. COMMANDER ABILITIES

Abilities consume energy (max 100). Energy is earned from kills.

EMP BLAST 1 (Cost: 40, Cooldown: 15s)
Freeze enemies in radius for 5 seconds. Frozen targets take 20% more damage.
OVERCLOCK 2 (Cost: 25, Cooldown: 10s)
Target one tower for +100% fire rate during a 10-second boost window.

08. AUDIO & SAVES

  • Adaptive Audio: 15 rotating normal melodies with dynamic threat mode.
  • Pause Audio Controls: music, SFX, and master mute are persisted in local storage.
  • Save System: supports auto-save and manual FORCE SAVE.

09. COMMAND CENTER (DEVELOPER ACCESS)

  • +1M CREDITS: inject 1,000,000 credits instantly.
  • NEW RIFT: force one additional rift.
  • LEVEL UP RIFT: tier-up a random existing rift.
  • +1 / +5 / +10 WAVE: advance waves while applying wave progression systems.
  • REBUILD RIFTS: clear and regenerate rift topology for the current wave target.
