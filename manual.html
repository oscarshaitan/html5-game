NEON DEFENSE

OPERATIONS MANUAL V2.0

01. MISSION OBJECTIVE

Protect the CORE at all costs. Enemies will spawn from rifts across the grid and attempt to reach the sector center. If 20 enemies breach your defenses, the system fails.

02. CONTROLS

  • Q / W / E - Select Tower Type (Basic, Rapid, Sniper)
  • Left Click - Place Tower / Select Entity
  • U - Upgrade selected tower
  • Del / Backspace - Sell selected tower
  • Esc - Deselect or Pause
  • Mouse Drag - Pan Camera
  • Mouse Wheel - Zoom in/out

03. THE CORE (BASE)

Your base is more than just a target. It is a functional defense platform.

  • Level 1-10: The Core can be upgraded to generate defense drones and a protective shield.
  • Repair: You can restore lost lives by repairing the base. Note: The price increases for every life purchased beyond the initial 20.
  • Defense Drones: Higher levels add more drones and layered shields.

04. ARSENAL

Basic Tower
Reliable all-rounder. Low cost ($50). Consistent damage and range.
Rapid Tower
High fire rate ($120), but lower range and damage per shot. Ideal for swarms.
Sniper Tower
Extreme range and high damage ($200). Very slow firing rate. Perfect for Bosses.

05. EVOLUTION & DIFFICULTY

As waves progress, the difficulty escalates. Keep an eye on incoming threats:

BASIC
Standard threat.
FAST
High velocity unit.
TANK
High HP armor.
BOSS
Heavy assault unit.
  • Rifts: New paths for enemies will open every 10 waves (every 5 waves after wave 50).
  • Mutations: Every 20 waves, enemies may undergo mutations, gaining unexpected traits.
  • Bosses: Every 10 waves, a Heavy Boss unit will emerge.
