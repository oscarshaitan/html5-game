NEON DEFENSE

OPERATIONS MANUAL V2.0

01. MISSION OBJECTIVE

Protect the CORE at all costs. Enemies will spawn from rifts across the grid and attempt to reach the sector center. If 20 enemies breach your defenses, the system fails.

02. CONTROLS

  • Q / W / E - Select Tower Type (Basic, Rapid, Sniper)
  • Left Click - Place Tower / Select Entity
  • U - Upgrade selected tower
  • Del / Backspace - Sell selected tower
  • Esc - Deselect or Pause
  • Tap Wave Counter - View Wave Intelligence
  • Mouse Drag - Pan Camera
  • Mouse Wheel - Zoom in/out

03. THE CORE (BASE)

Your base is more than just a target. It is a functional defense platform.

  • Level 1-10: The Core can be upgraded to generate defense drones and a protective shield.
  • Repair: You can restore lost lives by repairing the base. Note: The price increases for every life purchased beyond the initial 20.
  • Defense Drones: Higher levels add more drones and layered shields.

04. ARSENAL

Basic Tower
Reliable all-rounder. Low cost ($50). Consistent damage and range.
Rapid Tower
High fire rate ($120), but lower range and damage per shot. Ideal for swarms.
Sniper Tower
Extreme range and high damage ($200). Very slow firing rate. Perfect for Bosses.

  • Rifts: New paths for enemies will open every 10 waves (every 5 waves after wave 50). Click a Rift to view its Rift Intel.
  • Veteran Rifts: After Wave 50, Rifts have a 10% chance to Evolve into a higher Tier. Each Tier (T2, T3...) significantly boosts enemy HP, Speed, and Rewards.
  • Temporal Mutations: Every 20 waves, a random Rift will undergo a Temporal Mutation. During that wave, 100% of enemies from that Rift will be ultra-powerful variants (CRIMSON, VOID, etc.). Mutations are fleeting and clear after the anomaly wave.
  • Bosses: Every 10 waves, a Heavy Boss unit will emerge. After Wave 50, beware of Surprise Bosses (25% chance every 5 waves).

06. AUDIO SECTOR

Adjust your sensory experience in the Pause Menu:

  • Cyclic Melodies: The sector now features 15 unique melodies that rotate every wave, providing a diverse auditory backdrop to your defense operations.
  • Music Volume: Adjust the intensity of the 8-bit dynamic soundtrack.
  • SFX Volume: Control the volume of explosions, shots, and UI feedback.
  • Master Mute: Silence all audio for stealth operations.
  • Persistence: Your audio levels are automatically saved and restored between sessions.

07. COMMAND CENTER (DEVELOPER ACCESS)

For testing and sector evaluation, authorized commanders can access the COMMAND CENTER within the Pause Menu:

  • +1M CREDITS: Instantly inject 1,000,000 credits into the local economy.
  • TACTICAL SPAWN: Force-spawn any enemy type (Basic, Fast, Tank, Splitter, Bulwark, Shifter, or Boss) at a random Rift for defensive stress-testing.

08. TACTICAL SUPPORT

As Commander, you have access to powerful active abilities powered by SECTOR ENERGY. Energy is gained for every enemy unit destroyed on the grid.

EMP BLAST 1 (Cost: 40)
Deploys a localized EMP. Freezes enemies for 5 seconds. Stunned units take 20% more damage.
OVERCLOCK 2 (Cost: 25)
Target a tower to grant 100% fire rate boost for 10 seconds.
  • ENERGY: You earn 1 Unit per kill. Max capacity is 100 Units.
